Brownie

Paola Carosella. Brownie. YouTube, uploaded by Paola Carosella https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MVIBW_P1fCs.

Pudim

Paola Carosella. Pudim. YouTube, uploaded by Paola Carosella https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MVIBW_P1fCs.

This video is great too because it features one of the best chefs from Latin America. Madame Saiko is a pro in patisserie and teaches how to make a gourmet pudim, also a very popular sweet in Brazil.
